# Legal Risk Review Skill

## Procedure

1. Review the draft protocol row by row.
2. Identify blockers, serious risks, manageable risks and watch items.
3. For each blocker, state a concrete correction or unblock condition.
4. Check that proposed wording matches the stated rationale.
5. Check that fallback positions do not undermine non-negotiables.

## Common Failure Modes

- proposed wording is too vague to enforce;
- rationale says one thing while wording does another;
- protocol creates a new business obligation accidentally;
- fallback gives away the key protection;
- missing definitions make the change ineffective;
- inconsistent terminology across rows.

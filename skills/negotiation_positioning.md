# Negotiation Positioning Skill

## Procedure

1. Identify the user's deal goal.
2. Separate must-have protections from preferences.
3. Estimate counterparty resistance when possible.
4. Define fallback positions.
5. Recommend sequencing for negotiation.

## Priority Labels

- `must_have`: unacceptable to sign without a material change.
- `important`: should negotiate, but fallback is possible.
- `negotiable`: useful improvement, not a deal blocker.

## Guardrails

Do not turn legal uncertainty into business certainty. If the business impact is
unknown, ask for context.
